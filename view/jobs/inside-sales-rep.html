Compensation

Inside Sales Representative receives a guaranteed hourly rate of $10/hour. Average target compensation: $20-30/hour.

Flexible schedules available

Department

Call Center/Inside Sales

Summary of Functions

Providing "wow" to our Customers on a daily basis by providing education and awareness on Nexsense Products.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

  • Build relationships with customers over the phone.
  • Listen attentively to customer needs and concerns and recommend solutions.
  • Educate customers on product and service offerings.
  • Maintain extended family repository and sales tracking systems.
  • Other duties as assigned, including participating in our work hard, play hard culture.
  • Reports directly to the Inside Sales Manager.
  • Flexible schedules available.

Qualifications

  • Previous experience selling over the phone in a call center setting is preferred.
  • Previous experience with business-to-consumer sales.
  • Solid communication and interpersonal skills
  • Computer proficiency in Windows environment.
  • Experience with tracking business in CRM systems, preferably salesforce.

Physical Demands

  • Regular use of the telephone and e-mail for communication is essential.
  • Sitting for extended periods of time.
  • Hearing, vision and speaking within normal ranges.
  • No heavy lifting is expected. Exertion of up to 10 lbs. of force occasionally may be required.
  • Good manual dexterity for the use of common office equipment such as computer terminals, calculator, copiers and FAX machines.

Work Environment

Our Inside Sales Reps work indoors in a far-from-traditional office and call center environment. Activities include an occasional practical joke, chili cook off, or spur-of-the moment indoor golfing competition.
