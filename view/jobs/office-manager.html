Essential Duties and Responsibilities

  • Assists in some human resource-related work including on-boarding paperwork, interviews, and managing New Employee Orientation.
  • Coordinates with compliance department regarding solicitor permits. Helps Representatives complete applications and submits them to appropriate government agencies.
  • Assists Sales Manager with sales incentives.
  • Manages the front desk including greeting customers, interview candidates, and scheduling appointments.
  • Answers phones and arranges travel accommodations when necessary
  • Assists Corporate Office with Payroll procedures.
  • Makes arrangements and assists with executive management meetings, conferences and interviews. Maintains conference room schedule.
  • Handles ingoing and outgoing mail.
  • Orders office supplies and manages inventory of supplies.
  • Runs errands, orders and picks up company lunches.
  • Duties or projects as required or as assigned.

Qualifications

  • Must be proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel, Powerpoint and Outlook.
  • At least 2 years previous demonstrated office experience in a similar role.
  • Basic organizational skills are required for filing and collating paperwork.
  • Access to personal car to run company errands.

Physical Demands

  • Ability to withstand many different weather conditions.
  • Hearing, vision and speaking within normal ranges.
  • No heavy lifting is expected. Exertion of up to 10 lbs. of force occasionally may be required.
  • Good manual dexterity for the use of common office equipment such as computer terminals, calculator, copiers, and FAX machines.

Work Environment

The job is performed indoors in a traditional office and call center setting. Activities include extended periods of sitting with an occasional practical joke. Must thrive in a work-hard play-hard culture.
